More about Undertale

The gameUndertaleis a role-playing game developed independently by Toby Fox with additional art by Temmie Chang in the Game Maker: Studio engine.

The game is about two races ruled over the Earth: Humans and Monsters. One day, a war broke out between the two races and after a long series of battle, the humans were victorious. Thegraetestof their magicians confined the monsters to theUndergourndwith a magic barrier. However, this barrier is not perfect.

Key Characters in Undertale

Chara

Chara, also known as thefallen human, is the only other human character in the game, besides Frisk the protagonist, and was the first of the race to fall into the Underground.

Toriel

Toriel is amotherly figurecreated as an embodiment of excessive video game tutorials, she is a member of the monster race with hanging ears, small horns, white fur, and a purple robe.

Undyne

Undyne is a major character in Undertale, serving as a major character in the Neutral Route (ranging anywhere from a major protagonist, a posthumous character, or a major antagonist) and True Pacifist Route and the heroicsecondary antagonistof the Genocide Route
